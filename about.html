About Taarini

I'm Taarini Das — a creative strategist and storyteller working at the intersection of systems, power, and human dignity.

I come from political communication, civic innovation, and brand strategy. I've made content for a city government, built a brand around sexual wellness from scratch, and written a thesis about why awareness alone was never going to be enough. The through-line across all of it is the same: noticing when a system that claims to serve people is actually failing them, and redesigning for dignity instead of just access or information.
