AWARENESS

Is Not Enough.

Algorithms are designed to keep you hooked. I spent a year figuring out how to unhook — and what design owes us once we do.

The question

I looked at how algorithms are designed to keep us hooked, and how we actually unhook ourselves — not just from the apps, but from the anxiety they leave behind. Awareness of the problem was never the hard part. Everyone already knows their phone is designed to be addictive. The real question was what happens after you know that, and whether design has any responsibility once it does.
